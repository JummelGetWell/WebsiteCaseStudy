Getting Started

How to Use the GSO Borrower Portal

The General Service Office (GSO) Borrower Portal allows university students, faculty, and staff to borrow equipment and resources for academic and extracurricular activities.

Navigation Overview

  • Home: Dashboard with current borrowings, pending requests, and category browsing
  • Browse Items: Search and filter all available items by category
  • My Borrowings: View and manage your currently borrowed items and pending requests
  • Request History: Review past borrowing activities and request items again
  • Help: Access guides, FAQs, and support resources (you are here)

User Account

Your account is automatically created with your university credentials. Your borrowing history, requests, and account details are linked to your university ID.

Borrowing Process

How to Request Items
  1. Find an Item: Browse categories or use the search function to find the item you need
  2. Submit a Request: Click the "Request" button and complete the form with:
    • Your department or organization
    • Purpose for borrowing
    • Dates needed (from and until)
    • Any additional notes or requirements
  3. Approval Process: GSO staff will review your request, typically within 24-48 hours
  4. Pickup: Once approved, you can pick up the item from the designated GSO location

Note: Some high-value or specialized equipment may require additional approval from department heads or faculty advisors.

Returns & Extensions

Returning Items & Requesting Extensions

Returning Items

All items must be returned by their due date to avoid penalties.

  1. Go to "My Borrowings" tab to view your current items
  2. Click the "Return" button next to the item you wish to return
  3. Complete the return form, indicating the return location and item condition
  4. Return the item to the selected location
  5. A GSO staff member will process your return and update the system

Requesting Extensions

If you need to keep an item longer than originally requested:

  1. Go to "My Borrowings" tab
  2. Click the "Extend" button next to the item
  3. Submit your requested extension date and reason
  4. Wait for GSO approval (check your university email for notifications)

Important: Extension requests must be submitted at least 24 hours before the due date. Approval depends on whether the item is reserved by another user.

Account Management

Managing Your Account

Profile Information

Your profile is linked to your university account. If you need to update personal information, please contact the university registrar's office.

Borrowing Limits

Default borrowing limits are:

  • Students: Maximum 5 items at once, up to 14 days
  • Faculty/Staff: Maximum 10 items at once, up to 30 days
  • Student Organizations: Special limits based on needs and event requirements

Penalties

Late returns or damaged items may result in:

  • Temporary suspension of borrowing privileges
  • Fees for repairs or replacement
  • Academic holds (in severe cases)

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Can I reserve items in advance?

Yes, you can request items up to 30 days in advance. For special events, you can request up to 90 days in advance with department approval.

What if an item is damaged when I receive it?

Report any damages immediately upon receiving the item. Take photos if possible and contact the GSO office within 24 hours of pickup.

Can I cancel my request?

Yes, you can cancel pending requests at any time. Go to "My Borrowings," find the pending request, and click "Cancel."

How do I know if my request is approved?

You will receive an email notification when your request status changes. You can also check the status in the "My Borrowings" section.

What if I need training to use specialized equipment?

For specialized equipment, the GSO offers training sessions. You'll be notified if training is required when your request is approved.

Borrowing Guide

Borrowing Tips

Best Practices

  • Plan ahead: Request items at least one week before you need them
  • Be specific: Provide detailed information about your purpose and needs
  • Check accessories: Verify all components are included during pickup
  • Return on time: Return items promptly to avoid penalties and help others

Common Item Categories

  • Electronics: Laptops, tablets, cameras, adapters
  • Audio Visual: Projectors, microphones, speakers
  • Event Supplies: Tents, tables, chairs, decorations
  • Sports Equipment: Balls, nets, training gear
  • Lab Equipment: Microscopes, measurement tools

Policies & Rules

GSO Borrowing Policies

General Policies

  • All borrowers must be current students, faculty, or staff
  • Items may only be used for university-related activities
  • Borrowers are responsible for any damages or losses
  • Personal ID must be presented during pickup
  • Equipment must be returned in the same condition as received

Restricted Items

Some items have additional requirements:

  • High-value equipment requires faculty approval
  • Vehicles require special training and licensing
  • Laboratory equipment may require safety certification

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

System Access Problems

  • Can't log in: Verify your university credentials are current; contact IT Help Desk if issues persist
  • Missing borrowing history: Try clearing your browser cache or use a different browser
  • Request form not submitting: Check all required fields are completed

Equipment Issues

  • Item doesn't work: Contact GSO immediately for replacement or repair
  • Missing components: Take inventory at pickup; report issues within 24 hours
  • Damage during use: Report immediately; provide details of the incident
